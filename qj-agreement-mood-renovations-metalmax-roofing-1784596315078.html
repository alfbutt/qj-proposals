7. Guarantee Mechanics

7.1 Performance Guarantee

Quality Jobs guarantees that within the initial 90 Days term, Mood Renovations / Metalmax Roofing will achieve a minimum of $100,000 AUD in verified renovation work from leads generated through the programme. Based on the volume and quality of leads delivered, Mood Renovations / Metalmax Roofing can reasonably expect to receive 30 job quotes during this period.

7.2 One-Month Revenue Call-Off Period

Upon completion of the initial 90 Days term, a 1-month call-off period will commence automatically. During this month Mood Renovations / Metalmax Roofing continues on the standard management fee and Quality Jobs continues all services in full. This period exists solely to allow leads and quotes generated during the programme to convert into confirmed, invoiced revenue — ensuring Mood Renovations / Metalmax Roofing receives full credit for all pipeline work produced.

7.3 "Work for Free" Commitment

If, after the initial 90 Days term plus the one-month call-off period, the $100,000 AUD revenue benchmark has still not been reached, Quality Jobs will continue managing and optimising all campaigns at no additional management fee for a further 1 month.

  • Guarantee applies to management fees only — ad spend continues to be paid directly by Mood Renovations / Metalmax Roofing
  • If the benchmark is achieved during the free extension, no further extension is required

7.4 Ongoing Monthly Retainer

Following the completion of the initial 90 Days term, this agreement will continue on a rolling monthly basis at a retainer of $2000 INC GST. Either party may terminate the ongoing arrangement by providing 30 days written notice at any time.

8. Reporting

Monthly reports include: total leads, qualification notes, booked appointments, quotes issued, estimated pipeline value, and conversions (where available).
