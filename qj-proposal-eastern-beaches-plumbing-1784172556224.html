→Jeff Eastern Beaches - July 15

→Meeting Purpose
→To explore how Quality Jobs can help Jeff grow his bathroom renovation business.
→Key Takeaways
→- Jeff (Eastern Beaches Plumbing) wants to grow his renovation work from ~1 job/2 months to 2–4/month, leveraging an established team of subcontractors.
→- Quality Jobs proposed a system to deliver 10+ pre-qualified quotes/month, targeting high-end Eastern Suburbs clients.
→- Jeff is highly interested (rated 8/10) and will decide on the Tier 2 plan ($1,800/mo + $1,450 ad spend) by early next week.
→Topics
→Jeff's Business & Goals
→- Current Work Mix:
→- 70% strata/real estate maintenance
→- 30% renovations (15% direct, 15% subcontracted)
→- Renovation Capacity:
→- Current: ~1 job every 2 months
→- Immediate Goal: 2 jobs/month
→- Long-term Goal: 4 jobs/month to support a dedicated team
→- Job Profile:
→- Average Price: ~$30k (excluding PC items)
→- Average Profit: ~$10k
→- Motivation:
→- Shift focus from lower-margin maintenance to higher-margin, more satisfying renovation projects.
→- Transition to a supervisory role by training new staff.
→Quality Jobs' Proposed Solution
→- Guarantee: 10+ pre-qualified job quotes per month.
→- Revenue Guarantee: $100k in renovation revenue within 3 months, or Quality Jobs works for free for an additional month.
→- Lead Generation:
→- Platform: Facebook & Instagram ads.
→- Content: High-quality video ads using Jeff's portfolio to attract premium clients.
→- Targeting: Geofencing (Botany to Vaucluse) and income-based filters.
→- Lead Qualification Process (Tier 2):
→1. Lead Form: Captures key project details (budget, timeline, scope).
→2. Price Education: Leads are informed of typical costs ($25k–$35k) to filter out price-sensitive inquiries.
→3. Deep Qualification: Quality Jobs calls leads to confirm details, assess motivation, and book quotes directly into Jeff's calendar.
→Investment & Decision
→- Ad Spend: $1,450/month ($50/day), paid directly to Facebook.
→- Service Fees:
→- Tier 1: $1,300/month + GST (ads only; Jeff calls leads).
→- Tier 2 (Recommended): $1,800/month + GST (ads + full qualification service).
→- Jeff's Decision: Highly interested (8/10) and will review the proposal before deciding on the Tier 2 plan.
