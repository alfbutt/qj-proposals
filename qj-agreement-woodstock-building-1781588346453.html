2. Purpose

Woodstock Building engages Quality Jobs to provide a full managed Advertising, Lead Generation, Qualification and CRM Eco-System using Meta advertising systems for Renovation and building project acquisition.

They're not leveraging social media advertising despite having portfolio work to showcase, and they want to achieve 12 months of advance bookings to stabilize their business and income.

7. Guarantee Mechanics

7.1 Performance Guarantee

Quality Jobs guarantees that within the initial 3 months term, Woodstock Building will achieve a minimum of $120,000 AUD in verified renovation work from leads generated through the programme.

7.2 One-Month Revenue Call-Off Period

Upon completion of the initial 3 months term, a 1-month call-off period will commence automatically. During this month Woodstock Building continues on the standard management fee and Quality Jobs continues all services in full. This period exists solely to allow leads and quotes generated during the programme to convert into confirmed, invoiced revenue — ensuring Woodstock Building receives full credit for all pipeline work produced.

  • All services, campaigns and the vetting team continue to run as normal during the call-off month
  • Revenue confirmed and invoiced during the call-off month counts toward the $120,000 AUD target
  • Standard management fee remains payable for the call-off month

7.3 "Work for Free" Commitment

If, after the initial 3 months term plus the one-month call-off period, the $120,000 AUD revenue benchmark has still not been reached, Quality Jobs will continue managing and optimising all campaigns at no additional management fee for a further 1 month.

  • Guarantee applies to management fees only — ad spend continues to be paid directly by Woodstock Building
  • If the benchmark is achieved during the free extension, no further extension is required

* The complimentary extension applies to campaign management and optimisation services only. Receptionist and lead vetting team services are excluded and remain billable at the standard agreed rate during any extension period.
